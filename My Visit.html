Extend your stay in Canada as a visitor

If you want to extend your stay in Canada, you must apply to do so. Check your temporary resident status to make sure you apply before the expiry date. This will be on your study or work permit, the stamp in your passport or, if you do not have a stamp in your passport, 6 months from the day you entered Canada. You should apply 30 days before your status expires to: 1)extend your visitor, student or work status, 2)change your permit type (for example, from a study permit to a work permit) or 3)change your permit condition(s) (for example, if you are studying and you want to change schools, or if you are working and you want to change jobs).
